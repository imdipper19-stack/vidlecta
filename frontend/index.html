Frequently Asked Questions

What video formats are supported?

We support most common video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and WebM. Audio files like MP3, WAV, and M4A are also supported. Maximum file size is 1GB.

How accurate is the transcription?

Our AI achieves 95%+ accuracy for clear audio in English and Russian. The quality depends on audio clarity and background noise. You can always edit the transcription after processing.

How long does processing take?

Processing time depends on video length. Typically, a 60-minute video takes 5-10 minutes to transcribe. You'll receive an email notification when it's ready.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All uploads are encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't share your data with third parties. You can delete your data at any time from your account settings.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
